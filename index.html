PhD Candidate - Computer Science
Working on Music AI

       

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the School of Computer Science and Technology at the Xi'an Jiaotong University, supervised by Prof. Xinyu Yang. I am also a research intern at AMAAI Lab at SUTD hosted by Prof. Dorien Herremans from Nov. 2021 to Aug. 2022. Before that, I obtained my B.S. degree in Computer Science from Xi'an Jiaotong University in 2015. In addition, I conducted an exchange visiting program at Zhejiang University for six months during my undergraduate studies.

My research interest is mainly on deep generative networks and their applications on Music/NLP. Please refer to google scholar for details.

  • Music Information Retrieval
  • Symbolic Music Generation
  • Poetry Generation

My research is supported by the Shaanxi Provincial Art Research Institute, China Scholarship Council, Singapore MOE, and I am working closely with Huawei Corporation on music emotion recognition, singing quality evaluation.
